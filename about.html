About Us

For 119 years, Chirakkekaren Glass House has been the leading name in hardware, plywood, glass, and interior materials in the Thrissur district of central Kerala. Our rich legacy and unwavering trust have made us the go-to destination for all your building and interior needs.

Our Legacy

Founded over a century ago, Chirakkekaren Glass House has grown from a small establishment to a prominent retailer with three thriving shops. Our long-standing presence in the market is a testament to our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Our Products

With an extensive range of over 30,000 products, we cater to diverse needs and preferences. Whether you are looking for the finest hardware, premium plywood, or top-notch interior materials, we have it all. Our product categories include:

  • Hardware: From basic tools to advanced fixtures, we offer a comprehensive selection of hardware products.
  • Plywood: Discover a variety of plywood options that combine durability and aesthetic appeal.
  • Glass: Choose from an array of glass products that meet the highest standards of quality and craftsmanship.
  • Interior Materials: Elevate your interiors with our exquisite collection of materials designed to enhance any space.
